About Olivia Rodrigo

Olivia Isabel Rodrigo (born February 20, 2003) is an American singer-songwriter and actress.
She began her career as a child, appearing in commercials and the direct-to-video film An American Girl: Grace Stirs Up Success (2015).
She rose to prominence for her roles as a guitarist in the Disney Channel series Bizaardvark (2016-2019) and a
musical theater enthusiast in the Disney+ series High School Musical: The Musical: The Series (2019-2022).
